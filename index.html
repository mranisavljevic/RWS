Rainier Waste Solutions is a highly motivated company that is committed to offering the best service in order to help our customers achieve their goals effectively and efficiently.

Services

  • Roll-Off Hauling

    We service a variety of roll-off container styles in and around the Seattle area. We provide both scheduled and on-call delivery, removal, and collection of roll-off containers. All debris is taken to a recycling facility for responsible disposal.

    We offer roll-off hauling services for large and small demolition projects, commercial and residential construction jobs, permanent compactors, special event services, and more. Contact us for a quote.

    We collect the following materials:

    • Construction, demolition, and land-clearing debris
    • Asphalt, brick, concrete, stone, masonry, and dirt
    • Cardboard & Paper
    • Metals
    • Plastics
    • Unpainted, untreated wood
    • Gypsum wallboard scraps
    • Roofing materials and siding
    • Clean stryrofoam
    • Insulation
    • Carpet

  • Cardboard Bale Hauling

    We offer free collection of baled cardboard from distribution centers, grocery stores, packing companies, and all other types of businesses. We offer guidance and advice in purchasing balers for managing loose cardboard to allow for lower-cost collection. Contact us to find out how.

  • Waste Auditing

    We can help expand recycling programs to best fit the needs of businesses, and can offer a detailed plan for minimizing solid waste ultility bills by identifying opportunities to divert recycleable or compostable items from the garbage containers.

    For LEED-EB certification we provide thorough waste audits, and will provide all of the required documentation needed for certificate applications and renewals. Contact us to see how we may help.

  • Cart Delivery & Removal

    We have extensive experience with cart assembly, delivery, and removal programs, and offer these services to municipalities, waste companies, and cart manufacturers. Contact us to see how we can assist you with your project.

  • Container Labeling

    In order to cut costs and prolong cart and container lifespan we can help with large-scale relabeling projects. Contact us for a quote.

  • Route Auditing & Sequencing

    We utilize our years of waste industry experience to help improve routing efficiency and to identify potential savings in labor and fuel. Contact us for a quote.

  • Container Service & Maintenance

    We repair carts and steel containers, and can offer preventative maintenance in the delivery yard as well as in the field. Contact us for a quote.

  • Steel Container Painting

    We can paint new and existing steel containers to keep them functional and ready for customer sites. We offer full container painting as well as graffiti abatement. Contact us for a quote.

  • Gravity Lock Installation

    We install gravity locks on new and used containers. Contact us for a quote.

  • Litter Mitigation

    We can help you with large or small-scale events such as street festivals, races, and outdoor concerts. We deliver carts and litter stations for all waste streams to event location, and will continue to monitor and service those carts throughout the event to prevent overflow and litter. At the end of the event we ensure carts are removed promptly and the event area is properly cleaned. Contact us for a quote and availability.

  • Inventory Management

    We offer assistance in inventorying carts, containers, and other assets, and can create detailed plans for continued management and organization of inventory. Contact us to see how we can assist you with your project.

About Us

Rainier Waste Solutions is a Pacific Northwest company based in Seattle, WA. We offer a wide range of services that support and contribute to the evolving waste industry.

Our company started in 2009 with the delivery of garbage carts and containers to residents and businesses throughout the City of Seattle, and we have continued to expand our offerings since then. Chris Bridges, Owner and President, has extensive knowledge of the waste industry and has been working with major waste companies such as Waste Management, Republic Services, Waste Connections and Recology CleanScapes for over 15 years. Today, Rainier Waste Solutions' main area of focus is hauling in and around the Seattle area. Additionally, we believe education is vital in the fast-paced and ever-changing world of waste. Therefore, we offer waste audits along with educational programming for our customers so they can continue improving their environmental impact by disposing of waste appropriately and responsibly.
